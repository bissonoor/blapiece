About BLAPIECE

About Us & Our Skills

OUR VISION At BLAPIECE, we are driven by the power of community building and use fashion, art and technology as a means of connecting and sharing experiences with incredible people from around the world.

1000 ARTISTS We believe every single person has something unique to offer. Every week, we scout and curate a group of interesting people to join our mission of collective art making at the BLAPIECE mansion, our hub of creativity. The BLAPIECE team has created games that enable people - especially those who have never done art before - to paint, collaborate and bring out the artist inside them. Art works produced in these sessions are used as designs to make clothes that are as original as the people behind them. During our paint parties, we use live 3D to help people see their creations come to life in real-time, projected onto screens and worn by robot models. So far, 500 people have joined us on our journey to collaborate with 1000 artists.

VOICES FROM THE COMMUNITY The BLAPIECE community is a network of creatives, innovators and business executives. Our community keeps us inspired and pushes us to do inspirational things. Although Mito, an entrepreneur from Turkey, had never painted as an adult, he came to a paint session and told us it gave him “that specific positive feeling I remember from childhood.” Lily, a photographer from the UK, said “seeing your art on the clothes and them looking so good is really exciting and makes the group feel proud of what they have produced.”

TECHNOLOGY We are technologically-driven. BLAPIECE’s Model Ambassador is a robot named Derek and his female robot counterpart is currently being created. Our clothes are designed using 3D modeling and we get genuinely excited about the future. That’s why we’ve built a world of all things BLAPIECE in digital form: our metaverse in Cryptovoxels. It’s a place where people can interact with the brand, visit our art gallery and enjoy an immersive shopping experience from anywhere in the world. We accept payments in 7 different cryptocurrencies and have our own BLAPIECE TOKEN, a parachain built on Substrate and linked to the Polkadot network, to do our part in catalyzing the token economy.

WHAT'S NEXT? BLAPIECE has big plans. We are currently connecting with brands to form collaborations that will see our designs rebrand a wide range of products, from electric bikes to furniture. Our vision is to spread the transformative power of our community both digitally and physically by working with different groups of people and establishing an art residency in Bali. After the success of our recent painting sessions in Indonesia and the UAE, we will continue expanding globally. Feel free to ask us about the business model of our collaborations or our future plans of expansion – we would be more than happy to share what we have in store with you.

Our Services

Collaborative Art Experiences

Blapiece offers unique collaborative art experiences, bringing together artists from diverse backgrounds to co-create stunning artworks. These collaborative painting sessions take place at conferences,exhbitions or through virtual paint event, guided by some of our art creative director. Artists whether seasoned or newcomers, get to express their creativity, explore new techniques, and connect with a global community of like-minded individuals.

Limited Edition Fashion Designs

Blapiece transforms the collaborative artworks into limited edition fashion designs, making them wearable works of art. These exclusive fashion items, such as t-shirts, hoodies, silky shirts, kimonos, and more, embody the fusion of art and fashion. Each piece is thoughtfully crafted to reflect the individuality and creativity of the artists involved in the collaboration, offering customers a unique and expressive way to showcase their personal style.

NFT Collectibles and Digital Fashion

Blapiece embraces the world of blockchain technology and NFTs, transforming its art pieces and fashion designs into digital collectibles. By minting these NFTs, Blapiece creates a digital ownership experience, allowing art enthusiasts to own and trade these unique digital assets. Additionally, Blapiece delves into digital fashion, leveraging 3D modeling and virtual fashion shows to bring these creations to life in the metaverse, pushing the boundaries of wearable art and technology.
